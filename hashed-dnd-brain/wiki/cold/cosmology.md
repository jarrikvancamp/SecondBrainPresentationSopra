# Cosmology — Gist (L2)

Ball of knowledge; fine per-plane detail dropped — rehydrate from `archive/` if needed.

- **Great Wheel:** Material Plane at the hub, Inner (Elemental) planes ring it, Outer planes form the alignment wheel; Ethereal + Astral are the transitive passages.
- **Material + echoes:** Material Plane (mortal worlds); **Feywild** (vivid, emotion-shaped; archfey Domains of Delight); **Shadowfell** (bleak mirror; Domains of Dread, e.g. Barovia/Strahd).
- **Transitive:** Ethereal (Border + Deep, overlaps the Material) and Astral (silvery void of thought; route to the Outer Planes).
- **Inner / Elemental:** Air, Earth, Fire, Water + para-elemental fringes + the Elemental Chaos.
- **Outer (alignment-mapped):** ~17 planes; notably the **Nine Hells** (Lawful Evil, 9 layers, Asmodeus/Avernus, devils) and the **Abyss** (Chaotic Evil, endless layers, demon lords) — the two wage the eternal **Blood War**.
