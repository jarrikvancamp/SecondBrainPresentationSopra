# Iconic Monsters — Gist (L2)

Ball of knowledge; fine per-monster detail dropped — rehydrate from `archive/` if needed.

- **Aberrations:** Aboleth (CR 10, psychic *Enslave*, ancestral memory); Beholder (CR 13, antimagic cone + 10 eye rays); Mind Flayer (CR ~7, *Mind Blast*, extract brain, elder-brain colonies).
- **Undead:** Lich (CR 21, phylactery-anchored, 20th-level caster); Vampire family (CR 5–21, regeneration, *Misty Escape*, sunlight/stake weaknesses).
- **Apex:** Tarrasque (CR 30, reflective carapace, near-unkillable — needs *Wish*).
- **Dragons:** true dragons CR 1–24; breath by colour (Red fire / Blue lightning / Green poison / Black acid / White cold), metallics add a second breath; Ancients gain frightful presence + legendary/lair actions.
- **Staples:** Dragon Turtle (CR 17, steam breath); Mimic (CR 5, adhesive ambush); Owlbear (CR 3, *Rend*); Kobolds (pack tactics, sunlight sensitivity); Goblins (*Nimble Escape*); Gnolls (*Rampage*, Yeenoghu).
