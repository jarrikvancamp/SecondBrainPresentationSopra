Combat is structured play divided into rounds (~6 seconds each), during which every participant takes a turn in Initiative order.

## Step-by-Step Combat

1. **Establish Positions** — DM places all combatants.
2. **Roll Initiative** — Everyone makes a Dexterity check; results determine turn order for the entire encounter. Ties among players are resolved by the players; the DM resolves all other ties.
   - *Surprise:* A combatant caught unaware rolls Initiative with Disadvantage.
3. **Take Turns** — Each combatant acts in Initiative order; after everyone acts, a new round begins.
4. **End Combat** — Combat ends when one side is defeated, surrenders, or both sides agree to stop.

## Action Economy (per turn)

| Resource | Limit | Notes |
|----------|:-----:|-------|
| **Move** | Once | Up to your Speed in feet; can split before/after actions. |
| **Action** | Once | The main thing you do (attack, cast a spell, dash, etc.). |
| **Bonus Action** | Once | Only if a feature, spell, or rule specifically grants one. |
| **Reaction** | Once per round | Triggered on your turn or another's; resets at your turn start. |
| **Free Object Interaction** | Once | Minor thing during move or action (e.g., open a door). Additional interactions require the Utilize action. |

## Standard Actions

| Action | Summary |
|--------|---------|
| Attack | Make one or more weapon or Unarmed Strike attacks. |
| Dash | Gain extra movement equal to your Speed for the rest of the turn. |
| Disengage | Your movement doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks for the rest of the turn. |
| Dodge | Attack rolls against you have Disadvantage; you have Advantage on Dex saves. Lost if Incapacitated or Speed is 0. |
| Help | Grant Advantage on another creature's ability check or attack roll, or stabilize a creature. |
| Hide | Make a Dexterity (Stealth) check to become hidden. |
| Influence | Make a Charisma or Wisdom check to change an NPC's attitude. |
| Magic | Cast a spell, use a magic item, or activate a magical feature. |
| Ready | Declare a trigger and a response; use your Reaction to act when the trigger occurs. |
| Search | Make a Wisdom (Insight, Medicine, Perception, or Survival) check. |
| Study | Make an Intelligence check to recall information. |
| Utilize | Use a nonmagical object. |

## Making an Attack

1. Choose a target within range.
2. Determine modifiers (cover, advantage/disadvantage, bonuses/penalties).
3. Roll an attack roll ([[playing-the-game|D20 Test]]): d20 + ability modifier + Proficiency Bonus (if proficient).
4. If total ≥ target's **Armor Class (AC)** → hit; roll damage.
   - Natural 20 = Critical Hit (roll damage dice twice).
   - Natural 1 = automatic miss.

**Base AC (no armor):** 10 + Dexterity modifier. Armor, shields, spells, and features can modify this.

## Cover

| Degree | AC & Dex Save Bonus | Provided By |
|--------|:---:|-------------|
| Half Cover | +2 | Another creature or object covering at least half the target |
| Three-Quarters Cover | +5 | Object covering three-quarters of the target |
| Total Cover | Can't be targeted | Object fully covering the target |

Only the most protective cover applies; they don't stack.

## Opportunity Attacks

- **Trigger:** A creature you can see leaves your melee reach voluntarily (using its own movement).
- **Reaction:** Make one melee attack against that creature.
- **Avoid:** Take the Disengage action, teleport, or be moved by an external effect (no OA triggered).

## Movement in Combat

- **Difficult Terrain:** Costs 1 extra foot per foot moved.
- **Ranged attacks within 5 ft of a hostile enemy:** Made with Disadvantage.
- You can pass through an ally's space or a creature two sizes larger/smaller; you can't end your move in another creature's space.
