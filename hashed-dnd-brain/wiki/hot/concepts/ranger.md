# Ranger

The Ranger is a wilderness expert who weaves together combat skill, nature
magic, and survival knowledge into a self-reliant, adaptable adventurer.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Dexterity and Wisdom |
| **Hit Die** | d10 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Strength, Dexterity |
| **Spellcasting** | Nature half-caster (Wisdom-based, prepared spells) |

## Role

A versatile warrior comfortable at range or in melee, with nature magic that
enhances mobility and utility rather than raw damage. Rangers suit players who
want a rugged, independent character capable of both fighting and exploration
without relying on a single approach to encounters.

## Subclasses — Ranger Conclave

Rangers join a Conclave whose traditions shape how they draw on the bond
between martial skill and the natural world.

- **Beast Master** — Forges a deep magical bond with an animal companion that
  fights alongside the ranger and grows in power over time.
- **Fey Wanderer** — Draws on the otherworldly energy of the Feywild, mixing
  enchanting wonder with fierce fey fury.
- **Gloom Stalker** — Specializes in hunting enemies who rely on darkness,
  becoming invisible to darkvision and ambushing foes before they can act.
- **Hunter** — Adopts a wide toolkit of versatile combat techniques for
  protecting the wilds against any type of prey.
