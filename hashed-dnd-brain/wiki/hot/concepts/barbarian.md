# Barbarian

The Barbarian is a front-line martial class that channels raw fury to become a
devastating melee combatant, trading finesse for raw durability and overwhelming
physical force.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Strength |
| **Hit Die** | d12 (highest of any class) |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Strength, Constitution |
| **Spellcasting** | None |

## Role

The Barbarian enters Rage to boost damage output and resist incoming hits. A
Strength-focused front-liner who absorbs punishment while dealing heavy blows —
well-suited to players who want straightforward, impactful combat with minimal
spell management.

## Subclasses — Primal Paths

Barbarians choose a Primal Path that channels their Rage into a distinct form of
power.

- **Path of the Berserker** — Leans into pure aggression, unleashing unchecked violence
  at the cost of exhaustion.
- **Path of the Wild Heart** — Draws on a spiritual bond with the animal world,
  manifesting bestial traits and instincts.
- **Path of the World Tree** — Taps into the cosmic energy of the World Tree, granting
  resilience and reach across planes.
- **Path of the Zealot** — Fuses martial fury with divine conviction, raging in service
  of a patron deity.
