# Goblinoids

Goblinoids are a family of related Fey creatures — Goblins, Hobgoblins, and Bugbears — all sharing a common ancestry and cultural connections. In the 2024/2025 edition of D&D, all three are classified as **Fey** rather than Humanoids, reflecting their origins in the wild magic of the Feywild. Despite their shared lineage, the three kinds differ dramatically in temperament, tactics, and social structure.

See also: individual Goblin-focused coverage at [[goblins]] (if it exists).

## Goblins

**Size:** Small — **Role:** Swarm skirmishers and ambushers

Goblins are small, opportunistic, and numerous. They rely on speed, hit-and-run tactics, and sheer weight of numbers rather than individual power. The 2025 MM includes stat blocks for the Goblin Minion (expendable rank-and-file), the Goblin Warrior (standard combatant), the Goblin Boss (a more capable leader), and the Goblin Hexer (arcane threat). Goblins excel at Nimble Escape — disengaging or hiding as a bonus action — making them frustratingly evasive.

## Hobgoblins

**Size:** Medium — **Role:** Disciplined soldiers and battlefield commanders

Hobgoblins are larger, more intelligent, and highly militaristic. They organize into companies with clear ranks and execute coordinated battle plans. The 2025 MM presents the Hobgoblin Warrior, Hobgoblin Captain, and Hobgoblin Warlord, scaling from standard infantry to a legendary-action commander capable of rallying entire formations. A key hobgoblin trait is **Martial Advantage** — bonus damage when an ally stands adjacent to the target — rewarding tight formation tactics.

## Bugbears

**Size:** Large — **Role:** Lurking ambush predators

Bugbears are the largest and most physically imposing goblinoids, built for violence and stealth in equal measure. They are loners or small-gang hunters rather than soldiers. The 2025 MM offers the Bugbear Stalker (a scout optimized for surprise) and the Bugbear Warrior (a bruiser in open combat). Bugbears deal significantly more damage on a surprise attack, incentivizing patient ambush play.

## How They Differ

| Trait | Goblin | Hobgoblin | Bugbear |
|---|---|---|---|
| Size | Small | Medium | Large |
| Strength | Low | Medium | High |
| Tactics | Mob / escape | Military formation | Solo ambush |
| Intelligence | Cunning | Disciplined | Brutish but stealthy |
| Typical CR range | 1/4 – 1 | 1/2 – 4 | 1 – 3 |
