The four Elemental Planes — Air, Earth, Fire, and Water — form the Inner Planes, ringing the [[material-plane|Material Plane]] and supplying the raw elemental substance from which all physical worlds are made. See [[cosmology|Cosmology]] for how they fit the Great Wheel.

## Overview

Each Elemental Plane is a vast realm suffused entirely with one fundamental element. Near the Material Plane, the elements mingle in recognisable ways; deeper in, the pure element dominates to a degree hostile to life from other planes. At the outermost reaches, the four planes bleed into one another in the **Elemental Chaos** — a churning, unstable borderland.

Between adjacent Elemental Planes lie the **Para-elemental Planes**: Ash (Fire/Air), Ice (Air/Water), Magma (Fire/Earth), and Ooze (Earth/Water).

## The Four Planes

### Plane of Air
An endless open sky of shifting winds, cloud banks solid enough to support towns, and floating earth-motes covered in vegetation. Storms range from thunderstorms to hurricanes. The Labyrinth Winds make navigation treacherous. Djinn rule here; aarakocra are common inhabitants. The Wind Dukes of Aaqa guard against elemental evil from their hidden silver-spired realm.

### Plane of Earth
A titanic chain of mountains honeycombed with caverns. Light comes from mineral veins and bioluminescence, not any sun. The **Dao** (earth genies) are the dominant power, ruled from the City of Jewels in the Great Dismal Delve. Bordering regions shade into the Para-elemental Planes of Magma (the Furnaces) and Ooze (the Mud Hills).

### Plane of Fire
A blazing realm beneath a golden sky lit by a sun that waxes and wanes on a 24-hour cycle — blazing white at noon, deep red at midnight. The landscape is dominated by the Cinder Wastes (rivers of lava, obsidian ruins) and the **Sea of Fire**, on whose shores stands the **City of Brass** — the legendary efreeti metropolis, the greatest trading hub in the Inner Planes. Efreet, azers, salamanders, and red dragons inhabit this plane.

### Plane of Water
An infinite ocean called the **Sea of Worlds**, dotted with coral reefs and contested islands. Storms are frequent and extreme. The Marids' court resides in the opulent **Citadel of Ten Thousand Pearls**. The deepest reaches — the Darkened Depths — are crushed by pressure and cold, home to krakens and leviathans. Bordering regions include the Sea of Ice (near Para-elemental Ice) and the Silt Flats (near Para-elemental Ooze).

## Common Inhabitants

Genies (djinn, dao, efreet, marids), mephits, elementals, azers, salamanders, xorn, aarakocra, and gargoyles all have roots in the Inner Planes and can survive on the Material Plane.

## Elemental Chaos

At the farthest edges of the Inner Planes, the four elements collide in an endless, uncontrolled tumult. Elementals venture here briefly but typically return to the stability of their home planes.

## Role in the Cosmology

The Elemental Planes provide the *material* of the multiverse in the same way the Outer Planes provide its *purpose*. The [[ethereal-plane|Ethereal Plane]] overlaps all four and serves as the standard route into them from the Material Plane.
