The *Player's Handbook (2024)* is the core player-facing rulebook for the 2024 revision of Dungeons & Dragons 5th edition, covering character creation, game mechanics, and spells.

## Who It's For

Players and Dungeon Masters who want the complete rules for playing and creating characters. It assumes no prior D&D experience but is also designed as an upgrade for existing 5e players.

## Chapter-by-Chapter Outline

| Chapter | Title | What It Covers |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| Intro | Welcome to Adventure | What D&D is, what you need to play, overview of changes from 2014. |
| 1 | Playing the Game | Core mechanics: [[playing-the-game\|D20 Tests]], [[ability-scores-and-skills\|ability scores & skills]], [[combat\|combat]], exploration, social interaction. |
| 2 | Creating a Character | Step-by-step character creation: class, background, species, ability scores, advancement. |
| 3 | Character Classes | Full rules for all 12 classes (Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard) and their 48 subclasses. |
| 4 | Character Origins | Species (10 options) and Backgrounds (redesigned to grant ability score boosts and a feat). |
| 5 | Feats | Redesigned feat catalog; many feats are new or updated; backgrounds grant an Origin feat. |
| 6 | Equipment | Armor, weapons (with new Mastery properties), adventuring gear, tools, and crafting rules. |
| 7 | Spells | Spell descriptions for all classes; includes new and updated spells. |
| App. A | The Multiverse | Overview of D&D's cosmology and published campaign settings. |
| App. B | Creature Stat Blocks | Stat blocks for creatures characters can befriend or transform into. |
| App. C | Rules Glossary | Alphabetical definitions of every rules term; the canonical reference during play. |
