# Character Options (2024)

A character's **origin** = species + [[backgrounds|background]]. Together they define creature type, traits, ability scores, starting feat, and gear.

## Species

**2024 key change:** Species no longer grant ASIs — those move entirely to [[backgrounds|backgrounds]]. Species give creature type (Humanoid), size, speed, and traits only.

| Species | Size | Speed | Key Traits |
|---------|------|-------|-----------|
| **Aasimar** | M/S | 30 | Darkvision 60, Healing Hands, *Light* cantrip, Celestial Revelation (Wings/Radiance/Shroud) |
| **Dragonborn** | M | 30 | Draconic Ancestry (sets breath weapon type + resistance), Draconic Flight at 5 |
| **Dwarf** | M | 30 | Darkvision 120, poison resistance + Adv., +1 HP/level, Stonecunning (tremorsense 60) |
| **Elf** | M | 30 | Darkvision 60, Elven Lineage (High/Wood/Drow sub-type + spells), Fey Ancestry, Trance |
| **Gnome** | S | 30 | Darkvision 60, Gnomish Cunning (Adv. Int/Wis/Cha saves vs. magic), Gnomish Lineage |
| **Goliath** | M | 35 | Giant Ancestry (choose giant power), Large Form (1 min/long rest), Powerful Build |
| **Halfling** | S | 30 | Brave, Nimbleness (move through larger spaces), Lucky (reroll 1s), Naturally Stealthy |
| **Human** | M/S | 30 | Heroic Inspiration after long rest, bonus skill proficiency, extra Origin feat at level 1 |
| **Orc** | M | 30 | Adrenaline Rush (Dash + temp HP, PB×/long rest), Darkvision 120, Relentless Endurance |
| **Tiefling** | M/S | 30 | Darkvision 60, Fiendish Legacy (Abyssal/Chthonic/Infernal → spells + resistance), Thaumaturgy |

## Backgrounds

Backgrounds are the **sole source of ASIs** in 2024. Every background grants five things:

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Ability Score Increases** | Three scores listed; +2 one and +1 another, OR +1 all three. Cap 20. |
| **Origin Feat** | One Origin-category feat; no prerequisites, taken at level 1. |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Two specified skills. |
| **Tool Proficiency** | One specified tool (or choice from Artisan's Tools). |
| **Starting Equipment** | Themed gear package — or 50 GP to spend freely. |

**16 PHB 2024 backgrounds:** Acolyte, Artisan, Charlatan, Criminal, Entertainer, Farmer, Guard, Guide, Hermit, Merchant, Noble, Sage, Sailor, Scribe, Soldier, Wayfarer. Pre-2024 backgrounds can be converted via Chapter 2 guidelines.

## Feats

Gained two ways: one **Origin feat** from background at level 1; additional feats via class Ability Score Improvement features. A feat can only be taken once unless marked **Repeatable**.

| Category | When | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| **Origin** | Level 1 via background | No prerequisites |
| **General** | Class ASI feature | May have score/class prerequisites |
| **Fighting Style** | Class features | Specialized combat techniques |
| **Epic Boon** | ~Level 20 | Legendary capstone abilities |

**Origin feat examples:**

| Feat | Summary |
|------|---------|
| Alert | Initiative bonus; can't be surprised while conscious |
| Lucky | 2 Luck Points/long rest; reroll d20, pick either result |
| Magic Initiate | 2 cantrips + 1 level-1 spell (chosen class); cast spell 1×/long rest free; Repeatable | 
| Skilled | Proficiency in 3 skills or tools; Repeatable |
| Tough | +2 HP max per level |
| Tavern Brawler | Unarmed 1d4, reroll one damage die, improved grappling |
| Healer | Stabilize/restore HP with kit; improved Medicine |
| Crafter | 3 Artisan's Tools; 20% discount on non-magical items |

**General feat examples:** Ability Score Improvement (+2/+1+1; Repeatable), War Caster (Conc. saves + somatic with weapons + spell OA), Sentinel, Polearm Master, Sharpshooter (ignore cover, no long-range Disadv.), Great Weapon Master (+PB damage with heavy, bonus attack on crit/kill), Elemental Adept (Repeatable). See [[spellcasting]] for spell-access feats.

**Epic Boon examples:** Boon of Fate (±2d4 reaction), Boon of Fortitude (+40 HP max), Boon of Truesight (60 ft), Boon of Spell Recall (cast spell 1–4 without slot, 1×/long rest).

## Equipment

Sold gear = half price; trade goods (gems, art objects) = full value.

### Currency

| Coin | Abbr | GP Value |
|------|------|---------|
| Copper | CP | 1/100 |
| Silver | SP | 1/10 |
| Electrum | EP | 1/2 |
| Gold | GP | 1 |
| Platinum | PP | 10 |

50 coins = 1 lb.

### Weapons

**Simple vs. Martial** — proficiency granted by [[classes|class]]. **Melee** (5 ft reach) vs. **Ranged** (normal/long range; Disadv. beyond normal, no attack beyond long).

Key properties: Finesse (use Str or Dex), Light (enables dual-wield), Heavy (Disadv. if Str/Dex < 13), Reach (+5 ft), Versatile (1H or 2H damage), Thrown, Two-Handed, Loading, Ammunition (½ recoverable).

**Weapon Mastery (2024 new):** Requires a class feature (e.g., Warrior). Each weapon has one mastery:

| Mastery | Effect | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Cleave | Free attack vs. adjacent target on hit (no ability mod) | Greataxe |
| Graze | On miss, deal ability-mod damage | Greatsword |
| Nick | Dual-weapon extra attack in Attack action (not Bonus Action) | Dagger |
| Push | Push target 10 ft on hit | Greatclub |
| Sap | Target Disadv. on next attack | Longsword |
| Slow | Reduce Speed 10 ft on hit | Javelin |
| Topple | Con save or Prone on hit | Quarterstaff |
| Vex | Adv. on next attack vs. that creature | Rapier |

**Sample weapons:** Dagger 1d4 piercing 2 GP (Finesse, Light, Thrown) · Quarterstaff 1d6/1d8 2 SP · Shortbow 1d6 25 GP · Longsword 1d8/1d10 15 GP · Greatsword 2d6 50 GP · Rapier 1d8 25 GP · Longbow 1d8 50 GP.

### Armor

| Category | AC | Dex Cap | Str Req | Don |
|----------|----|---------|---------|-----|
| Light | 11–12 + full Dex | — | — | 1 min |
| Medium | 12–15 + Dex | +2 max | — | 5 min |
| Heavy | 14–18 (fixed) | — | 13 or 15 | 10 min |
| Shield | +2 | — | — | Utilize action |

Some armor = Stealth Disadv. Wearing armor without proficiency → Disadv. on Str/Dex checks, attacks, saves, and blocks [[spellcasting]].

**Sample costs:** Leather 10 GP · Studded Leather 45 GP · Chain Shirt 50 GP · Breastplate 400 GP · Chain Mail 75 GP · Plate 1,500 GP · Shield 10 GP.

### Tools & Gear

Tool proficiency adds PB to checks; skill proficiency in same area grants Advantage. **Artisan's Tools** (16 types, 20–50 GP) for crafting. **Other tools:** Thieves' Tools 25 GP, Herbalism Kit 5 GP, Poisoner's Kit 50 GP, Disguise Kit 25 GP, Navigator's Tools 25 GP, Gaming Sets, Musical Instruments. Adventuring gear (rope, rations, torches, backpacks, etc.) listed by weight and cost in Chapter 6.
