# Magic Items & Treasure (2024)

## Rarities & Values

Consumables (except Spell Scrolls) are worth ~half listed value.

| Rarity    | Typical Value | Notes                          |
|-----------|--------------|--------------------------------|
| Common    | ~100 GP      | Many towns                     |
| Uncommon  | ~400 GP      | Cities                         |
| Rare      | ~4,000 GP    | Larger cities                  |
| Very Rare | ~40,000 GP   | Unusual vendors                |
| Legendary | ~200,000 GP  | Extraordinary sources          |
| Artifact  | Priceless    | Unique; story-bound; special destruction method required |

## Item Categories (9)

| Category       | Key Rule                                                      |
|----------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Armor          | Must be worn to function                                      |
| Potions        | Consumable; drinking = Bonus Action                          |
| Rings          | Must be worn on finger/equivalent digit                      |
| Rods           | Scepter-like; can serve as Arcane Focus                      |
| Scrolls        | Consumable (destroyed on use); any literate creature can try |
| Staffs         | Doubles as Quarterstaff + Arcane Focus                       |
| Wands          | Can double as Arcane Focus                                    |
| Weapons        | Magical; magic ammo counts as magical for rules              |
| Wondrous Items | Catch-all: bags, boots, cloaks, figurines, horns, etc.       |

Activation default = **Magic action** + item-specific interaction (command word, drinking, reading). Spells cast via item use lowest spell level, no components, no spell slots.

## Attunement

- Max **3 attuned items** at a time.
- Attunement requires a **short rest** bonding with the item.
- Ends automatically on death, curse removal, or extended separation from item.
- Some items have **prerequisites** (class membership or ability to cast ≥1 spell — not granted by another item).

## Charges & Recharge

- Charge count revealed by *Identify*; attuned creature always knows current count + daily regain.
- Typically recharge at **next dawn** (DM discretion on planes without dawn).
- Rolling max die for recharge can trigger permanent magic loss (item-dependent).

## Cursed Items

- Curse **not detectable** by *Identify* or standard identification; reveals itself on use or when conditions are met.
- Once attuned, character **cannot voluntarily end attunement** until curse is removed (e.g., *Remove Curse*).
- Effects vary: stat penalties, compulsions, compliance demands.

## Magic Item Award Guidelines

A typical party earns ~100 items by level 20:

| Tier (Levels) | Common | Uncommon | Rare | Very Rare | Legendary |
|:-------------:|:------:|:--------:|:----:|:---------:|:---------:|
| 1–4           | 6      | 4        | 1    | 0         | 0         |
| 5–10          | 10     | 17       | 6    | 1         | 0         |
| 11–16         | 3      | 7        | 11   | 7         | 2         |
| 17–20         | 0      | 0        | 5    | 11        | 9         |

Items are prizes, not necessities — characters don't need them to keep pace.

## Notable Items

**Bag of Holding** *(Wondrous, Uncommon, no attunement)* — Extradimensional sack: holds 500 lb / 64 cu ft, weighs only 15 lb. Ruptures if overfilled/punctured (contents to Astral Plane). ~10 min air for living creatures inside. Placing inside another extradimensional space (e.g., Portable Hole) destroys both and rifts everything within 10 ft to Astral Plane.

**Deck of Many Things** *(Wondrous, Legendary, no attunement)* — 22 illustrated cards; declare number to draw before drawing; each card resolves immediately with permanent effects ranging from ability score boosts, wishes, and property deeds to soul imprisonment (*The Void*), loss of all wealth (*The Ruin*), or cursed hunters. Card returns to shuffled deck after draw; tied to lore figure Euryale.
