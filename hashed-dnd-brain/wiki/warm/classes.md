# Classes (5e 2024)

The 2024 PHB defines 12 classes, each granting hit points, proficiencies, and a unique feature set that scales by level. Every class offers exactly **4 subclasses**, all chosen at level 3, representing distinct specialisations within the archetype.

## Spellcasting Categories

- **Full casters** (all levels count toward slot progression): Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard.
- **Half casters** (half levels count): Paladin, Ranger.
- **Subclass casters** (one-third levels, specific subclass required): Fighter (Eldritch Knight), Rogue (Arcane Trickster).
- **Pact Magic**: Warlock — few slots that recharge on a short rest, not the standard long-rest progression.
- **Non-casters**: Barbarian, Monk.
