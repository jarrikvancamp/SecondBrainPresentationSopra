Undead predators sustained by the blood and life force of the living, ranging from feral spawn to powerful noble lords.

**Role/type/CR:** Medium undead (shapechanger). The 2024 Monster Manual presents a full family of vampire stat blocks: Vampire Familiar (minor thrall), Vampire Spawn (CR 5), Vampire Nightbringer (CR 9), Vampire (CR 13, ~AC 16, ~144 HP), and Vampire Umbral Lord (CR 21). The full Vampire has legendary actions.

**Signature abilities**
- *Regeneration* — recovers HP at the start of each turn; halted only by sunlight, running water, or radiant damage that turn.
- *Misty Escape* — when brought to 0 HP away from its resting place, a vampire transforms into mist and retreats to its coffin rather than dying outright.
- *Charm* — a gaze-based effect that forces a Wisdom save; failure leaves the target charmed and willing to offer its blood.
- *Bite* — drains life force (reducing max HP) and heals the vampire for the same amount.
- *Spider Climb* — moves freely across ceilings and vertical surfaces.
- *Shapechanger* — can assume the form of a bat or a cloud of mist.

**Weaknesses**
Harmed by sunlight (radiant damage plus disadvantage on attacks each round), damaged by running water, repelled by garlic, unable to enter a dwelling without an invitation, and paralyzed by a wooden stake through the heart while resting in its coffin.

**Lore in brief**
A vampire creates spawn from humanoids it fully drains, building a network of enthralled servants; spawn are bound to obey their creator unless freed. The most ancient and powerful vampires can command entire shadow courts, accumulating influence over centuries while concealing their nature behind mortal identities.
