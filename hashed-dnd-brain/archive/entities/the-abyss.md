The Abyss is the Chaotic Evil plane of the [[cosmology|Outer Planes]] — a virtually limitless descent into corruption, entropy, and violence. It is the native realm of demons and the embodiment of chaos turned to destructive evil.

## Environment

Every layer of the Abyss presents its own horrific landscape, all sharing the plane's pervasive decay. Surfaces crumble, structures corrode, and the plane itself seems to be in a state of ongoing rot. The Abyss actively corrupts those who enter it, and opening a portal to it can let that corruption seep into surrounding areas, transforming them into Abyssal pockets.

## Layer Structure

Layers are numbered in the order they were catalogued by explorers from Sigil. The total count is effectively infinite.

| Layer | Name | Notable Detail |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Plain of Infinite Portals | Arrival point; iron fortresses, red sun, portals to other planes |
| 45–47 | Azzagrat | Graz'zt's three-layer realm of decadent courts and hidden dangers |
| 66 | The Demonweb | Lolth's realm of magical webbing concealing portals and dungeon passages |
| 88 | The Gaping Maw | Demogorgon's primeval jungle continent surrounded by endless ocean |
| 113 | Thanatos | Orcus's realm of graveyards, bleak moors, and swarms of Undead |
| 222 | The Slime Pits (Shedaklah) | Juiblex and Zuggtmoy's domain of ooze and fungi |
| 422 | The Death Dells | Yeenoghu's ravine-carved hunting grounds |
| 600 | The Endless Maze | Baphomet's infinite dungeon labyrinth |

## Inhabitants

Demons of every variety fill the Abyss, organised loosely under demon lords — powerful entities who each claim one or more layers. Unlike devils, demons follow no true hierarchy; the strongest simply dominate through raw power and terror.

## The Blood War

The Abyss is one of the two primary fronts of the **Blood War**, the eternal conflict pitting demonic hordes against the regimented devil legions of the [[nine-hells|Nine Hells]]. The war rages across both planes and spills into others.

## Role in the Cosmology

The Abyss represents chaos and evil at their most unrestrained. Adventures here confront adventurers with their own capacity for darkness and the temptation to abandon allies to survive. The plane's corruption can bleed into the Material Plane anywhere a portal to it exists.
