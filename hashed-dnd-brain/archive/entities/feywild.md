The Feywild — also known as the Plane of Faerie — is a parallel dimension occupying the same cosmological space as the [[material-plane|Material Plane]], serving as its vibrant, idealized reflection. See [[cosmology|Cosmology]] for the broader planar structure.

## Environment and Character

The Feywild is a world of heightened beauty and intensified emotion. The landscape responds to strong feelings: flowers recoil during arguments, grass withers beneath malice, and birds cheer for the joyful. Terrain echoes the Material Plane but in a more wondrous, colourful, and magical form — a muddy river becomes a brook sparkling like diamonds; a ruined fortress might be an archfey's soaring castle.

Time and geography are mutable. Roads shift, distances distort, and the plane's layout changes as frequently as the mood of its rulers.

## Inhabitants

The Feywild is home to **Fey** creatures of all kinds, ruled by powerful archfey who each hold a domain called a **Domain of Delight**. Notable domains include:
- **Summer Court** — ruled by Queen Titania, a pastoral realm of perpetual summer.
- **Gloaming Court** — ruled by the Queen of Air and Darkness, a twilight realm of instinct.
- **Prismeer** — the domain of the archfey Zybilna.

## Fey Crossings

Natural portals called **fey crossings** exist where the Material Plane and the Feywild nearly touch — a mushroom ring, a forest pool, or a clearing. Most open only under specific conditions (a full moon, a particular season) and close permanently if the surrounding landscape is drastically altered.

## Planar Hazards

- **Memory Loss** — Creatures leaving the Feywild may forget everything they experienced there unless they succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 10).
- **Time Warp** — Days spent in the Feywild may pass as minutes, weeks, or even years in the wider multiverse.

## Role in the Cosmology

The Feywild can also serve as a route between worlds of the Material Plane, though such paths are uncharted and unpredictable.
