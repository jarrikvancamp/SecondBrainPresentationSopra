# Feats (2024)

A feat is a special capability representing training, talent, or experience beyond what a character class provides. Feats are gained in two ways: every [[backgrounds|background]] grants one Origin feat at character creation, and class advancement periodically offers a feat instead of an Ability Score Improvement.

A feat can only be taken once unless its description explicitly states it is **Repeatable**.

## Feat Categories

Feats are divided into four categories:

| Category | When Taken | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| **Origin** | Character creation (from background) | No prerequisites; designed for level 1 |
| **General** | Class Ability Score Improvement feature | May have ability score or other prerequisites |
| **Fighting Style** | Class features that offer Fighting Styles | Represent specialized combat techniques |
| **Epic Boon** | Powerful rewards at very high levels (around 20th) | Represent legendary abilities |

## Parts of a Feat

- **Category** — one of the four above; must match when a specific category is required
- **Prerequisite** — a class level, ability score, or other requirement that must be met (unless a feature waives it); class prerequisites require at least 1 level in that class
- **Benefit** — the mechanical effect
- **Repeatable** — if absent, the feat can only be taken once

## Origin Feats (examples)

These have no prerequisites and are intended for level 1 characters:

| Feat | Effect Summary |
|------|---------------|
| **Alert** | Initiative bonus; can't be surprised while conscious |
| **Lucky** | 2 Luck Points per long rest; spend to reroll d20 (pick either result) |
| **Magic Initiate** | Learn 2 cantrips + 1 level 1 spell from a chosen class; cast the spell once/long rest for free (repeatable — one class per instance) |
| **Skilled** | Gain proficiency in three skills or tools of your choice (repeatable) |
| **Tavern Brawler** | Unarmed strikes deal 1d4; can reroll one damage die; improved grappling |
| **Tough** | HP maximum increases by 2 per level (current and future) |
| **Healer** | Use a Healer's Kit to stabilize or restore HP; improved Medicine checks |
| **Savage Attacker** | Once per turn, reroll weapon or unarmed strike damage and take either result |
| **Crafter** | Proficiency with three Artisan's Tools; 20% discount when buying non-magical items |
| **Musician** | Proficiency with three instruments; grant Heroic Inspiration to allies after a long rest |

## General Feats (examples)

| Feat | Effect Summary |
|------|---------------|
| **Ability Score Improvement** | +2 to one ability score, or +1 to two different scores (repeatable) |
| **War Caster** | Adv. on concentration saves; can perform somatic components with weapons/shields; can cast spells as Opportunity Attacks |
| **Sentinel** | Reaction attack when adjacent enemy attacks someone else; stops movement on hit |
| **Polearm Master** | Bonus attack with polearm butt; Opportunity Attack when enemy enters reach |
| **Sharpshooter** | Ignore half/three-quarters cover; no Disadvantage at long range |
| **Great Weapon Master** | Bonus attack on crit or kill; add PB to damage with heavy weapons |
| **Elemental Adept** | Choose damage type; spells of that type ignore resistance; treat 1s as 2s on damage dice (repeatable — different element per instance) |

## Fighting Style Feats (examples)

| Feat | Effect Summary |
|------|---------------|
| **Archery** | +2 to ranged attack rolls |
| **Defense** | +1 AC while wearing armor |
| **Great Weapon Fighting** | Reroll 1s or 2s on two-handed weapon damage dice |
| **Protection** | Use reaction + shield to impose Disadvantage on an attack against an adjacent ally |

## Epic Boon Feats (examples)

| Feat | Effect Summary |
|------|---------------|
| **Boon of Fate** | Add or subtract 2d4 from a creature's d20 roll (reaction), PB times/long rest |
| **Boon of Fortitude** | HP maximum increases by 40 |
| **Boon of Truesight** | Truesight 60 ft |
| **Boon of Spell Recall** | Cast any level 1-4 spell known/prepared without a slot (once/long rest) |
