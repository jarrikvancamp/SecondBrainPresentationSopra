# Rogue

The Rogue is a cunning, Dexterity-focused specialist who uses stealth, guile,
and precise strikes to deal devastating bursts of damage while staying out of
harm's way.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Dexterity |
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Dexterity, Intelligence |
| **Spellcasting** | None (Arcane Trickster subclass only) |

## Role

A skill-intensive character who rewards positioning and preparation. The Rogue's
Sneak Attack feature delivers high single-target damage when attacking with
advantage or alongside an ally, while Cunning Action and Uncanny Dodge keep
them mobile and hard to pin down. A strong fit for players who enjoy outmaneuvering
enemies rather than overpowering them.

## Subclasses — Roguish Archetypes

Rogues follow an Archetype that specializes their talent for trickery and
precision.

- **Arcane Trickster** — Supplements sneaky schemes with arcane spells, using
  illusions and enchantments to add a magical edge to infiltration.
- **Assassin** — Masters the opening strike, layering deadly poisons and
  ambush tactics to neutralize targets before they can respond.
- **Soulknife** — Manifests blades of pure psychic energy, gaining psionic
  abilities that enhance infiltration and combat without any equipment.
- **Thief** — Hones practical criminal skills to excel at breaking and entering,
  using objects with uncanny speed, and claiming treasure others cannot reach.
