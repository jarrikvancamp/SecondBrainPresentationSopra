# Spells Overview (2024)

The D&D spell system spans levels 0–9 across eight thematic schools of magic. Classes each have their own spell list; a spell's eligible classes are noted in parentheses after its school in the spell description. See [[spellcasting]] for how to cast, prepare, and limit spells.

## Spell Levels

| Level | Name | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| 0 | Cantrip | No slot required; can be cast at will |
| 1–9 | Leveled spells | Expend a slot of equal or higher level |

Higher-level slots produce stronger versions of spells that support upcasting.

## The Eight Schools of Magic

| School | Typical Effect |
|--------|---------------|
| **Abjuration** | Prevents or reverses harmful effects; wards and protective barriers |
| **Conjuration** | Moves creatures or objects across space; summons and teleportation |
| **Divination** | Reveals hidden information; foresight and detection |
| **Enchantment** | Influences, controls, or manipulates minds |
| **Evocation** | Channels raw energy into destructive or restorative effects |
| **Illusion** | Deceives the mind or senses; false images and phantom effects |
| **Necromancy** | Manipulates the boundary between life and death; undead and vitality |
| **Transmutation** | Alters the physical form of creatures or objects |

These categories are primarily descriptive. A few class features and abilities do reference them mechanically (e.g., an Abjuration Wizard's features keying on abjuration spells).

## Class Spell Lists (concept)

Each spellcasting class has a defined spell list. If a spell is on your class's list, you can prepare and cast it. Some features and feats (e.g., Magic Initiate) can grant access to spells outside your normal list, without making you a member of that class.

|-------|--------|----------------|
| Eldritch Blast | Evocation (Warlock) | Fires a beam of crackling energy dealing 1d10 force damage; multiple beams at higher levels |
| Fire Bolt | Evocation (Sorcerer, Wizard) | Hurls a mote of fire dealing 1d10 fire damage at range |
| Prestidigitation | Transmutation (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard) | Minor magical tricks: clean, soil, light candles, produce a trinket, etc. |
| Guidance | Divination (Cleric, Druid) | Touch a creature to add 1d4 to one ability check before the spell ends |
