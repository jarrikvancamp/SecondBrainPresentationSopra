# Wizard

The Wizard masters arcane magic through rigorous study and a spellbook,
commanding the broadest spell selection of any class at the cost of physical
fragility.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Intelligence |
| **Hit Die** | d6 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Intelligence, Wisdom |
| **Spellcasting** | Arcane full caster (Intelligence-based, spellbook) |

## Role

A preparation-heavy full caster who rewards players who learn the encounter
ahead of time and choose the right spells for it. The Wizard's spellbook can
grow by copying spells from scrolls and other books, providing access to the
widest variety of magical solutions in the game. Physically the most vulnerable
class, requiring careful positioning and smart play to avoid damage.

## Subclasses — Arcane Traditions

Wizards specialize in an Arcane Tradition that focuses their scholarship on a
particular school of magic.

- **Abjurer** — Concentrates on protective and banishing magic, creating wards
  that shield themselves and allies from harm.
- **Diviner** — Seeks knowledge of what has happened and what will come, using
  foresight and fortune-telling to shape outcomes in the party's favor.
- **Evoker** — Channels raw magical energy into explosive destructive effects,
  fine-tuning blasts to spare allies caught in the area.
- **Illusionist** — Weaves convincing illusions to deceive enemies, manipulate
  perception, and create opportunities through misdirection.
