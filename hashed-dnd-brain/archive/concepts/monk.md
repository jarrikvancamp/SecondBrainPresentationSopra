# Monk

The Monk is a highly mobile unarmed combatant who channels inner discipline into
rapid strikes and defensive techniques, darting in and out of melee to land
concentrated bursts of damage.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Dexterity and Wisdom |
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Strength, Dexterity |
| **Spellcasting** | None |

## Role

Monks use a resource pool (Discipline/Ki) to fuel their signature abilities — extra
attacks, enhanced movement, and stunning strikes. They require both Dexterity (for
attack and defence) and Wisdom (for key class features), making ability score
management important. High complexity suits players who enjoy tactical mobility.

## Subclasses — Warrior Traditions

Monks follow a Warrior tradition that expands how they apply their inner discipline.

- **Warrior of Mercy** — Channels focused energy through touch to either drain
  vitality from foes or restore it to allies.
- **Warrior of Shadow** — Harnesses darkness and shadow for stealth, misdirection,
  and surprising attacks.
- **Warrior of the Elements** — Commands elemental forces — air, earth, fire, and
  water — to extend and empower unarmed strikes.
- **Warrior of the Open Hand** — Masters pure unarmed technique, unlocking
  specialised grappling, push, and combat control options.
