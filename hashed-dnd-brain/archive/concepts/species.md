# Player Species (2024)

A character's species determines creature type, size, speed, and special traits. All ten PHB 2024 species are **Humanoid**. Characters of all species reach physical maturity at roughly the same age; most live about 80 years unless otherwise noted.

> **2024 key change:** Species no longer grant ability score increases. Those are now entirely provided by the character's [[backgrounds|background]]. This separates roleplay-driven species choice from mechanical optimization.

Each species provides a creature type (Humanoid), a size, a speed, and special traits. Some species allow choosing Small or Medium size.

## Shared Structure

Every species description in the book follows the same format:
- **Creature Type** — always Humanoid for PHB species
- **Size** — specified (some allow Small or Medium choice)
- **Speed** — base walking speed in feet
- **Special Traits** — unique abilities from physiology or magical nature

Playable non-Humanoid species (e.g., Centaur, Minotaur) appear only in supplemental books, not the core PHB.
