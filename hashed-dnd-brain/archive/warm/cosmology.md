## Overview — The Great Wheel

The D&D multiverse is organised as concentric wheels. The **Material Plane** (+ echo-planes) sits at the hub; the **Inner Planes** (Elemental) ring it; the **Outer Planes** form the outer wheel, arranged by alignment. The arrangement is metaphorical — no observer verifies it directly. Positive and Negative Energy Planes enfold the whole.

| Category | Members |
|---|---|
| Material Realm | Material Plane + echoes: Feywild, Shadowfell |
| Transitive | Ethereal Plane, Astral Plane — passages between other planes |
| Inner (Elemental) | Air, Earth, Fire, Water + Para-elemental planes + Elemental Chaos |
| Outer | 17 planes mapped to the nine alignments |

## Material Realm

| Plane | Key Traits | Inhabitants / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Material Plane** | Hub of mortal life; countless Prime Worlds in Wildspace systems; all are shards of a primordial First World, seeded by Yggdrasil. Travel between worlds requires spelljamming, portal, or transit through an echo. | Mortals of all kinds; Wildspace contains the suns/planets of each world |
| **Feywild** (echo) | Vivid, emotionally heightened reflection of the Material Plane. Landscape responds to emotion. Time/geography mutable — roads shift, distances distort. Memory loss on exit (Wis DC 10); time warp (days = minutes or years outside). | Fey creatures; archfey rule **Domains of Delight** (Titania/Summer Court; Queen of Air & Darkness/Gloaming Court; Zybilna/Prismeer). **Fey crossings** (mushroom rings, forest pools) are natural portals; close if surrounding land is destroyed. |
| **Shadowfell** (echo) | Dark, decayed mirror of the Material Plane. Featureless black sky, grasping forests, crumbling landmarks. Pervasive melancholy → **Shadowfell Despair** on extended stay (Wis DC 10; Disadvantage on rolls). | Undead, shadow dragons, cloakers, darkmantles. **Shadow crossings** (dark tombs, graves) seal shut in light. **Domains of Dread** deep within — isolated demiplanes ruled by Darklords imprisoned by the Dark Powers (Barovia/Strahd; Mordent; Falkovnia). |

## Transitive Planes

| Plane | Key Traits | Hazards / Notable Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Ethereal Plane** | Misty fog overlapping the Material Realm and all Elemental Planes. **Border Ethereal**: ghostly view 30 ft into adjacent plane, no interaction, no gravity. **Deep Ethereal**: roiling mist, visibility 30 ft; coloured vapour curtains are portals to specific planes (turquoise → Material, dusky grey → Shadowfell, opalescent → Feywild). Standard route to Inner Planes. | **Ether cyclones** (divert/scatter travellers or dump them on Astral). **Radiant Citadel** floats in the Deep Ethereal — a living city of diplomacy built on a fossil around the Auroral Diamond, linked to 27 Material Plane civilisations via Concord Jewels. |
| **Astral Plane** | Infinite silver-grey sea; realm of thought and dream. No gravity — movement by thought alone. **No ageing, hunger, or thirst.** Colour pools (coin-sized light discs) are gateways to Outer Planes (ruby → Nine Hells; amethyst → Abyss; gold → Mount Celestia). Standard route to Outer Planes. | **Psychic winds** (delay travel, scatter to random pools, psychic damage). **Dead god husks** drift through — githyanki hollow these for outposts; Tu'narath built inside one. **Wildspace** is where the Astral overlaps the Material; spelljamming vessels use the Astral as interstellar highway. Githyanki patrol aggressively with silver swords. |

## Inner Planes — Elemental

| Plane | Environment | Dominant Inhabitants |
|---|---|---|
| **Air** | Endless open sky; cloud banks solid enough for towns; floating earth-motes; treacherous Labyrinth Winds | Djinn; aarakocra; Wind Dukes of Aaqa guard against elemental evil |
| **Earth** | Vast cavern-riddled mountain chain; light from mineral veins; borders Magma (Fire side) and Ooze (Water side) para-elemental planes | Dao rule from the City of Jewels in the Great Dismal Delve; xorn |
| **Fire** | Cinder Wastes (lava, obsidian); **Sea of Fire**; 24-hr sun cycle (white noon → red midnight). **City of Brass** on the Sea of Fire = greatest Inner Planes trade hub. | Efreet (rulers); azers, salamanders, red dragons |
| **Water** | Infinite **Sea of Worlds** ocean; coral reefs, contested islands; **Darkened Depths** (crushing cold, krakens, leviathans); borders Sea of Ice and Silt Flats | Marids rule from **Citadel of Ten Thousand Pearls**; sea creatures |
| **Para-elemental** | Ash (Fire/Air), Ice (Air/Water), Magma (Fire/Earth), Ooze (Earth/Water) — border zones between adjacent elemental planes | Mixed inhabitants of each adjacent pair |
| **Elemental Chaos** | Outermost region where all four elements collide in uncontrolled tumult | Elementals visit briefly; largely uninhabitable |

### Nine Hells (Baator) — Lawful Evil — [[devils]]

Nine descending layers, each ruled by an archdevil under **Asmodeus** (Nessus, layer 9). Portals arrive only at **Avernus** (layer 1); **River Styx** threads all layers downward. Rigid devil hierarchy: lemures → imps → bearded → chain → bone → horned → erinyes → ice devils → pit fiends → archdevils; promotion/demotion wielded as control.

### The Abyss — Chaotic Evil — [[demons]]

Virtually infinite descending layers of corruption and entropy; pervasive decay corrodes structures and corrupts visitors. Portals to it can corrupt surrounding Material Plane areas. Demon lords rule by raw power — no true hierarchy. **Blood War** with the Nine Hells rages across both planes.

## Planar Travel

| Method | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Portals** | Stationary links; may need key item or command word. **Sigil** (City of Doors, atop the Outlands Spire) = greatest concentration in the multiverse. |
| **Spells** | *Plane Shift*, *Gate*, *Etherealness*, *Astral Projection* |
| **Transitive routes** | Ethereal → Inner Planes; Astral (via colour pools) → Outer Planes |
| **Planar roads** | Infinite Staircase; River Oceanus (Upper Planes); River Styx (Lower Planes); Yggdrasil the World Tree |
