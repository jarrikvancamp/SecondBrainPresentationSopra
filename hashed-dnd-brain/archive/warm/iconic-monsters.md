# Iconic Monsters — Consolidated Reference

See also: [[monster-groups]], [[monsters-overview]]

| Monster | Type | CR | Signature Abilities |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Aboleth** | Large Aberration | 10 | *Enslave* — persistent psychic domination, survives range; *Mucus Cloud* — contact disease that transforms victims' skin; inherits eons of ancestral memory, schemes across centuries. |
| **Beholder** | Large Aberration | 13 | *Antimagic Cone* — central eye continuously suppresses all magic in the cone it faces; *Eye Rays ×10* — each stalk fires a different ray (charm, fear, disintegrate, death, petrify, etc.); Legendary Resistance prevents easy shutdown. |
| **Lich** | Medium Undead | 21 | *Phylactery* — soul anchored to a separate object; destroyed body reforms in days, only permanently killed by destroying the phylactery first; *Paralyzing Touch* + 20th-level wizard spellcasting; Legendary Resistance ×3/day. |
| **Mind Flayer** | Medium Aberration | 7 (base) / 8 (Arcanist) | *Mind Blast* — recharge cone stuns targets on failed Int save for up to 1 min; *Extract Brain* — instantly kills incapacitated grappled humanoids; reproduces via ceremorphosis; colonies dominated by an elder brain. |
| **Tarrasque** | Gargantuan Monstrosity | 30 | *Reflective Carapace* — spell attacks and targeted spells bounce back at casters; *Multiattack* — bite (can swallow Huge creatures) + 2 claws + horn + tail; near-unkillable (Wish required to prevent return), Legendary Resistance ×3/day, immune to most elements. |
| **Vampire** | Medium Undead (Shapechanger) | 5–21 (family) | *Regeneration* — regains HP each turn unless hit by sunlight or radiant that round; *Misty Escape* — drops to 0 HP away from coffin → transforms to mist and retreats; Bite drains max HP; weaknesses: sunlight, running water, stake, uninvited entry. Spawn (CR 5) → Nightbringer (CR 9) → Vampire (CR 13) → Umbral Lord (CR 21). |
| **Dragons (True)** | Dragon (various sizes) | 1–24 | *Breath Weapon* (recharge 5–6) by color: Red=fire cone, Blue=lightning line, Green=poison cone, Black=acid line, White=cold cone; metallics add a second breath (sleep/repulsion/slow/weaken/paralyze). Adults/Ancients gain *Frightful Presence*, Legendary Actions, and Lair Actions. Gold/Red Ancient ≈ CR 24. |
| **Dragon Turtle** | Gargantuan Dragon | 17 | *Steam Breath* — scalding steam cone (fire damage, briefly obscures); heavily armored shell yields very high AC; Bite + Claws shred ships; neutral, demands tribute from passing vessels; no legendary actions. |
| **Mimic** | Medium Monstrosity (Shapechanger) | 5 | *Adhesive* — any creature that touches the object form is immediately grappled/adhered; *False Appearance* — indistinguishable from mundane furniture while still; Pseudopod + Bite (piercing + acid). |
| **Owlbear** | Large Monstrosity | 3 (std) / 6–9 (Primeval) | *Rend* — when both beak and claw hit the same target, deals bonus damage; *Screech* (Primeval only) — frightens nearby creatures; Keen Sight & Smell grants Perception advantage; purely instinct-driven, fiercely territorial. |
| **Kobolds** | Small Humanoid | 1/8–1/4 | *Pack Tactics* — advantage on attacks when an ally is adjacent to the target; *Sunlight Sensitivity* — disadvantage on attacks and Perception in sunlight; warrens riddled with traps; serve nearby dragons as worshippers. |
| **Goblins** | Small Humanoid | 1/8–1 | *Nimble Escape* — Disengage or Hide as a bonus action, making them slippery skirmishers; *Boss Redirect* — Goblin Boss deflects incoming attacks onto adjacent allies; Hexer variant adds curse/debuff spellcasting. |
| **Gnolls** | Medium Humanoid | 1/2–5 | *Rampage* — upon dropping any foe to 0 HP, makes a free bonus bite against a nearby creature, fueling a chain frenzy; Fang of Yeenoghu (CR 4) channels demonic power for extra bite attacks; devoted to Yeenoghu, no fixed lairs. |
