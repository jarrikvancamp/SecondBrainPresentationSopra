# Fighter

The Fighter is the quintessential weapon-and-armour specialist, proficient with
every weapon and armour type, and built around consistent, reliable damage output
over many rounds.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Strength or Dexterity |
| **Hit Die** | d10 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Strength, Constitution |
| **Spellcasting** | None by default (Eldritch Knight subclass gains spells) |

## Role

Fighters are the most straightforward martial class — their low complexity makes
them accessible, while Action Surge and Extra Attack ensure they remain effective
at all levels. Strength builds favour heavy armour and melee; Dexterity builds
suit finesse and ranged combat.

## Subclasses — Martial Archetypes

Fighters choose an Archetype that defines their battle style or adds new capabilities.

- **Battle Master** — Learns a set of tactical combat maneuvers that spend Superiority
  Dice to add special effects to attacks.
- **Champion** — Focuses on maximising raw martial effectiveness, pushing physical
  capabilities such as critical hit range to their peak.
- **Eldritch Knight** — Supplements weapon fighting with a selection of spells
  drawn from the wizard tradition to enhance combat effectiveness.
- **Psi Warrior** — Augments attacks and defences with psychic energy, using
  psionic power to move objects and shield themselves.
