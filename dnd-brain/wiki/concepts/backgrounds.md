# Character Backgrounds (2024)

A background represents the occupation and life circumstances most formative to a character before they became an adventurer. It is one of two components of a character's **origin** (the other being [[concepts/species|species]]).

> **2024 key change:** Backgrounds are now the sole source of ability score increases, replacing the old system where species granted them. This makes species choice flavor-first and gives all characters equally impactful backgrounds mechanically.

## What a Background Provides

Every background grants five things:

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Ability Score Increases** | Three ability scores are listed. Increase one by 2 and another by 1 — OR increase all three by 1. No score may exceed 20 this way. |
| **Origin Feat** | One specified feat from the Origin category (see [[concepts/feats]]). Taken at character creation, no prerequisites. |
| **Skill Proficiencies** | Proficiency in two specified skills. |
| **Tool Proficiency** | Proficiency with one specified tool, or one chosen from the Artisan's Tools category. |
| **Starting Equipment** | A package of thematically appropriate gear — or 50 GP to spend as the player prefers. |

## The 16 PHB Backgrounds

| Background  | Flavor / Role |
|-------------|---------------|
| Acolyte     | Temple servant; religious studies |
| Artisan     | Skilled tradesperson or craftworker |
| Charlatan   | Con artist, smooth-talker |
| Criminal    | Outlaw, thief, or underworld operative |
| Entertainer | Performer: bard, acrobat, jester |
| Farmer      | Rural laborer; close to the land |
| Guard       | Watchman or hired protector |
| Guide       | Wilderness scout or pathfinder |
| Hermit      | Solitary seeker of knowledge or peace |
| Merchant    | Trader, shopkeeper, or traveling vendor |
| Noble        | Aristocratic upbringing and privilege |
| Sage        | Scholar, researcher, or academic |
| Sailor      | Seafarer; experienced on ships and waterways |
| Scribe      | Clerk, record-keeper, or copyist |
| Soldier     | Trained military veteran |
| Wayfarer    | Wanderer, traveler, or homeless drifter |

## Using Older Backgrounds

If using a background from a pre-2024 book, the PHB 2024 provides conversion guidelines (see "Backgrounds and Species from Older Books" in Chapter 2) to apply the new ability-score-and-feat format.
