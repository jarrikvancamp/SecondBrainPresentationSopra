# Paladin

The Paladin is a heavily armored divine warrior who blends martial combat with
sacred magic, serving as both a frontline fighter and a support healer bound by
a holy oath.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Strength and Charisma |
| **Hit Die** | d10 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Wisdom, Charisma |
| **Spellcasting** | Divine half-caster (Charisma-based, prepared spells) |

## Role

A durable melee combatant who channels divine power through Smite attacks and
supportive auras. The Paladin's dual focus on Strength and Charisma rewards
players who want a powerful frontliner with healing, protection, and the
versatility of a prepared-spell list — all shaped by a chosen sacred oath.

## Subclasses — Sacred Oaths

Paladins swear a Sacred Oath that defines their divine calling and grants
additional spells and abilities.

- **Oath of Devotion** — Follows the disciplined ideals of angelic justice and
  righteousness, embodying the classic virtuous knight archetype.
- **Oath of Glory** — Strives for peak heroism and legendary deeds, inspiring
  companions to rise to extraordinary feats alongside them.
- **Oath of the Ancients** — Bonds with the natural world and the forces of life
  and joy, standing as a protector of light against encroaching darkness.
- **Oath of Vengeance** — Dedicates their power to pursuing and punishing those
  who commit great evils, prioritizing justice over mercy.
