# Druid

The Druid is a nature-attuned full caster who commands elemental forces, summons
natural allies, and can transform into animals, making them uniquely flexible in
both combat and exploration.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Wisdom |
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Intelligence, Wisdom |
| **Spellcasting** | Full caster (Wisdom-based) |

## Role

Druids wield a broad nature-themed spell list covering healing, control, and direct
damage, complemented by the Wild Shape ability to assume beast forms. Their complexity
rating is high — they reward players comfortable managing both spells and
shape-shifting options.

## Subclasses — Druid Circles

Druids join a Circle that focuses their connection to the natural world.

- **Circle of the Land** — Draws additional spells from a chosen terrain type,
  deepening the Druid's attunement to a specific natural environment.
- **Circle of the Moon** — Unlocks more powerful and combat-capable animal forms
  through Wild Shape, emphasising physical transformation.
- **Circle of the Sea** — Channels the restless power of ocean and storm,
  wielding water and lightning magic.
- **Circle of the Stars** — Adopts a starry spirit form that grants distinct bonuses
  tied to celestial constellations.
