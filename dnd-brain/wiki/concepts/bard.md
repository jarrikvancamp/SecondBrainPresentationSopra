# Bard

The Bard is a charismatic performer-mage who weaves magic through artistic
expression, excelling at supporting allies, hindering enemies, and accumulating
knowledge across many fields.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Charisma |
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Dexterity, Charisma |
| **Spellcasting** | Full caster (Charisma-based) |

## Role

Bards are versatile full casters with a broad spell list, skill expertise, and the
ability to bolster allies through Bardic Inspiration. They suit players who enjoy
flexibility and a mix of social, exploratory, and combat contributions.

## Subclasses — Bard Colleges

Bards join a College that shapes how they use their talents in the world.

- **College of Dance** — Channels agility and rhythm into defensive and offensive
  maneuvers, merging movement with spellwork.
- **College of Glamour** — Draws on the seductive power of the Feywild to charm,
  mesmerise, and beguile foes and bystanders alike.
- **College of Lore** — Prioritises scholarship and breadth of magical knowledge,
  collecting secrets and spells from many traditions.
- **College of Valor** — Combines martial weapons training with spellcasting,
  allowing the Bard to hold their own on the front line.
