# Warlock

The Warlock draws magical knowledge from an otherworldly patron, trading a
conventional spellbook for a pact that grants occult power through a distinctive
short-rest recharge system.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Charisma |
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Wisdom, Charisma |
| **Spellcasting** | Pact Magic (Charisma-based; few slots, recharge on short rest) |

## Role

A spellcaster built around powerful, frequently reused spell slots rather than
a large pool. Eldritch Blast serves as a reliable damage cantrip, while Eldritch
Invocations customize the character extensively. The patron theme and invocation
system reward players who enjoy building a unique, flavor-rich magical identity
through mechanical choices.

## Subclasses — Otherworldly Patrons

A Warlock's Patron is the supernatural being from whom they have obtained their
power, shaping the character's theme and expanded spell options.

- **Archfey Patron** — Receives gifts of fey teleportation and enchanting magic,
  channeling the capricious power of the Feywild.
- **Celestial Patron** — Borrows radiant and healing energies from a heavenly
  being, blending dark occult flavor with divine-adjacent support magic.
- **Fiend Patron** — Draws power from an infernal lord, gaining access to sinister
  fire-and-brimstone abilities along with durability through dark bargains.
- **Great Old One Patron** — Delves into the forbidden lore of ancient cosmic
  entities, wielding psychic power and forbidden knowledge.
