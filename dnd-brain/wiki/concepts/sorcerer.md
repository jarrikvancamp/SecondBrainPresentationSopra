# Sorcerer

The Sorcerer wields magic that flows naturally from within rather than from
study or a pact, reshaping raw innate power through metamagic to suit the moment.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Charisma |
| **Hit Die** | d6 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Constitution, Charisma |
| **Spellcasting** | Innate full caster (Charisma-based, known spells) |

## Role

A spontaneous caster with a smaller spell list than the Wizard but greater
flexibility in how spells are cast, thanks to Sorcery Points and metamagic
options. Constitution saves help maintain concentration spells under fire.
Best suited to players who want a focused, impactful magical identity that
reflects their character's supernatural origin rather than academic training.

## Subclasses — Sorcerous Origins

A Sorcerer's Origin reveals the source of their innate magical power and
unlocks additional spells and abilities tied to that lineage.

- **Aberrant Sorcery** — Taps into unsettling psionic energy linked to alien
  entities, bending the mind alongside the physical world.
- **Clockwork Sorcery** — Channels the rigid cosmic forces of order and
  mathematical precision, counteracting chaos and bolstering reliability.
- **Draconic Sorcery** — Inherits power from a draconic bloodline, gaining
  resilience and breath-weapon-themed magic tied to a dragon type.
- **Wild Magic Sorcery** — Lets untamed chaos surge through every spell,
  risking unpredictable magical effects that can aid or hinder.
