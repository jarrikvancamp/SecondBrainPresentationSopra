# Cleric

The Cleric is a divine conduit who draws power from a deity or cosmic force,
combining reliable healing and support magic with the ability to smite enemies and
bolster allies in equal measure.

## Class Statistics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **Primary Ability** | Wisdom |
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Saving Throw Proficiencies** | Wisdom, Charisma |
| **Spellcasting** | Full caster (Wisdom-based) |

## Role

Clerics are durable full casters who serve as a party's primary healer while
remaining capable combatants. Their Divine Domain subclass dramatically shapes
whether they favour healing, battlefield control, or direct damage. Good for players
who want meaningful choices between supporting and fighting.

## Subclasses — Divine Domains

Clerics choose a Divine Domain that reflects the aspect of their deity they embody.

- **Life Domain** — Specialises in powerful healing magic, bolstering allies and
  restoring Hit Points efficiently.
- **Light Domain** — Commands radiant and fire magic to scorch enemies and protect
  allies with warding light.
- **Trickery Domain** — Uses illusions, misdirection, and deceptive magic to
  confound and mislead foes.
- **War Domain** — Inspires martial courage in allies and punishes enemies, blending
  combat prowess with divine authority.
