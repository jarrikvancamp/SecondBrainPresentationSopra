The Ethereal Plane is a misty, fog-laden transitive dimension that overlaps the [[entities/material-plane|Material Plane]], the [[entities/feywild|Feywild]], the [[entities/shadowfell|Shadowfell]], and all four Elemental Planes simultaneously. It serves as the primary passage between the Material Realms and the Inner Planes. See [[concepts/cosmology|Cosmology]] for broader context.

## Two Regions

### Border Ethereal
The Border Ethereal overlaps its adjacent plane directly. Travellers here can peer into the overlapped plane (up to about 30 feet, appearing washed-out and grey) but cannot interact with it normally. Solid objects in the overlapped plane do not block movement in the Border Ethereal, and travellers are effectively invisible to those on the other side unless magic reveals them. Gravity is absent — movement is fully three-dimensional.

The *Etherealness* spell places a creature in the Border Ethereal.

### Deep Ethereal
Beyond the Border Ethereal lies the Deep Ethereal: roiling mist with visibility limited to roughly 30 feet. Coloured **curtains of vapour** appear throughout this region; passing through one transports the traveller to the Border Ethereal of a specific plane (e.g., turquoise for the Material Plane, dusky grey for the Shadowfell, opalescent for the Feywild).

Distance is meaningless in the Deep Ethereal; travel between curtains takes hours regardless of apparent distance.

## Hazards

**Ether cyclones** sweep through without warning, potentially diverting travellers, hurling them to a random Border Ethereal, or depositing them on the Astral Plane entirely.

## Notable Feature: The Radiant Citadel

Floating in the Deep Ethereal is the **Radiant Citadel** — a living city carved from a single massive fossil around a luminous gemstone called the Auroral Diamond. It is a cosmopolitan sanctuary of diplomacy and trade, connected to twenty-seven far-flung Material Plane civilisations via orbiting gem-ships (the Concord Jewels).

## Role in the Cosmology

The Ethereal Plane is the standard route to the Inner Planes (Elemental Planes). Travellers entering the Astral Plane from the Ethereal can reach the Outer Planes.
