# Giants

Giants are Huge or larger humanoid-shaped beings with tremendous physical power. They are classified as the Giant creature type and share a cultural and spiritual framework called the **Ordning** — a divinely ordained rank structure that determines each giant's social standing within and across the giant kinds.

## The Ordning

The Ordning places each type of giant in a ranked order from least to greatest. Hill Giants occupy the bottom; Storm Giants rule the apex. A giant's rank within its own kind is determined by physical prowess, wealth, or wisdom depending on the subtype. Interactions between different giant kinds are heavily shaped by where each falls in the Ordning.

## Giant Kinds (ranked low to high)

| Giant | CR | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Hill Giant | 5 | Lowest-ranked; gluttonous and dull-witted, driven mainly by appetite and brute force |
| Stone Giant | 7 | Cave-dwellers who are reclusive and philosophical; exceptional throwers of boulders |
| Frost Giant | 8 | Fierce, warlike raiders from frozen tundra; prize battle trophies and displays of strength |
| Fire Giant | 9 | Highly disciplined smiths and slavers who operate from volcanic strongholds; military in structure |
| Cloud Giant | 9 | Wealthy and aristocratic, dwelling in sky castles; some are arrogant and aloof, others more benevolent |
| Storm Giant | 13 | The most powerful and wisest of giants; prophetic, isolated, and closest to divine purpose |

## Encounter Notes

- Giants as a rule are not simply mindless brutes — most have culture, language, and social ambitions.
- Fire and Frost Giants are frequently encountered as antagonists due to their aggressive expansionism.
- Cloud and Storm Giants may act as reluctant allies or neutral powers depending on the narrative.
