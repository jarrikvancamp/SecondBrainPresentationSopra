A gargantuan engine of near-unkillable destruction — effectively the most dangerous creature in the material plane.

**Role/type/CR:** Gargantuan monstrosity, CR 30. Roughly AC 25 (natural armor), ~676 HP. Has legendary resistance (×3/day) and legendary actions; designed as a world-ending, campaign-capstone encounter.

**Signature abilities**
- *Reflective Carapace* — ranged spell attacks and certain line or single-target spells (such as magic missile) are reflected back at their caster, making magical bombardment risky.
- *Frightful Presence* — all creatures within 60 feet must succeed on a Wisdom save or be frightened, hampering their ability to fight back.
- *Multiattack* — combines a bite, two claw strikes, a horn attack, and a tail swipe in a single turn; the bite can swallow a Huge or smaller creature whole.
- *Siege Monster* — deals double damage to structures, making it capable of leveling fortifications with ease.
- *Legendary Resistance* — brushes off a limited number of failed saves each day.
- *Immunity* — cannot be charmed, frightened, or paralyzed, and is immune to most elemental damage types.

**Lore in brief**
The tarrasque is treated as a force of nature rather than a normal creature — it periodically rises from a deep slumber, tears across entire regions with mindless hunger, and cannot be permanently destroyed by conventional means (a *Wish* spell is typically required to prevent its return). Most settings treat its awakening as an extinction-level event.
