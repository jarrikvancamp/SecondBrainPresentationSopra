# Demons

Demons are Chaotic Evil fiends native to the Abyss, an infinitely layered plane of pure malevolence and ruin. Unlike their devil counterparts, demons follow no law and recognize no hierarchy beyond raw power — they rend, consume, and destroy for the pleasure of it. Demons carry immunity to fire, poison, and lightning, and the most powerful ones resist cold and physical damage from non-magical weapons.

## The Blood War

The Blood War is the endless, grinding conflict between demons and devils for supremacy over the Lower Planes. Demons fight with overwhelming numbers and chaos; devils counter with discipline and strategy. The war has raged for millennia with no end in sight. Adventurers who find themselves near the front lines face danger from both sides.

## Encounter Notes

- Demons rarely cooperate even with each other; a clever party can sometimes exploit internal rivalries.
- Summoned demons must be magically bound or they will turn on their summoner.
- Demon lords (not stat-blocked in the standard Monster Manual) rule individual Abyssal layers and represent CR 20+ threats.
