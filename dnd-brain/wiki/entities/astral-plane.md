The Astral Plane is an infinite silvery sea — a realm of thought, memory, and dream that serves as the transitive pathway between the [[entities/material-plane|Material Plane]] and the Outer Planes. See [[concepts/cosmology|Cosmology]] for the wider framework.

## Environment

The Astral Plane appears as a vast, featureless expanse of silver-grey luminescence shot through with white wisps of energy. Distant pinpricks of light are the stars of far-off Wildspace systems. The plane has no gravity — creatures move by the force of thought alone. Crucially, **no ageing occurs** and creatures feel neither hunger nor thirst here, making it a strange, timeless void.

## Navigation

Movement is purely mental: a traveller thinks about a destination and becomes aware of the direction to take. Travel time to a general destination (such as a colour pool leading to a specific plane) is roughly 1d4 × 10 hours; reaching a specific outpost may take several days.

## Key Features

### Colour Pools
Gateways to other planes manifest as shimmering, coin-like discs of coloured light (e.g., ruby for the Nine Hells, amethyst for the Abyss, gold for Mount Celestia). Finding the right colour pool is the standard way to reach an Outer Plane from the Astral.

### Dead Gods
The petrified remains of slain or forgotten deities drift through the Astral like enormous stone husks. The githyanki and mind flayers often hollow these out for cities and outposts; the infamous githyanki city **Tu'narath** is built within such a dead god.

### Wildspace
Where the Astral Plane overlaps with the Material Plane, vast airless regions called **Wildspace systems** emerge, containing the suns, planets, and moons of individual worlds. Spelljamming vessels navigate Wildspace and use the Astral as the highway between star systems.

## Hazards

**Psychic winds** are storms of lost memories and stray thoughts that batter travellers' minds. They can delay journeys, scatter travellers to random colour pools, or inflict psychic damage and unconsciousness.

## Notable Inhabitants

Githyanki maintain silver swords and patrol the Astral aggressively. Mind flayers, Celestials, Fiends, modrons, and slaadi can all be encountered crossing the plane on their own business.
