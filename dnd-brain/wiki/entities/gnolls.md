# Gnolls

**Type:** Medium Humanoid  
**Alignment:** Chaotic Evil

## Overview

Gnolls are hyena-headed humanoids driven by a relentless hunger and devotion to Yeenoghu, the demon lord of gnolls. They raid in packs and are infamous for their ability to sustain violent momentum through combat.

## Variants (2024 Monster Manual)

| Variant | Approx. CR | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Gnoll Warrior | 1/2 | Standard raider; bite and spear attacks |
| Gnoll Pack Lord | 2 | Commands gnoll groups; multiattack with whip |
| Gnoll Fang of Yeenoghu | 4 | Demonic devotee; extra bite attacks and demonic energy |
| Gnoll Demoniac | ~5 | Most powerful cultist; channeled fiendish power |

## Signature Traits

- **Rampage:** When a gnoll reduces an enemy to 0 HP, it can use a bonus action to make a bite attack against a nearby creature—fueling an escalating frenzy.
- **Pack Coordination:** Gnolls fight as a mob, and their leaders amplify surrounding warriors.

## Ecology & Behavior

Gnoll war bands serve Yeenoghu almost instinctively, leaving devastation in their wake. The Fang of Yeenoghu is a gnoll touched directly by demonic power, acting as a living extension of the demon lord's will. Gnolls rarely settle—they consume and move on.
