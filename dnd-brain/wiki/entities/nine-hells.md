The Nine Hells — also known as Baator — is the Lawful Evil plane of the [[concepts/cosmology|Outer Planes]] and the supreme domain of devils. It embodies evil disciplined by iron law, hierarchy, and the relentless pursuit of ambition through contract and deceit.

## Structure

The plane is organised into nine descending layers, each ruled by an **archdevil** answerable to **Asmodeus**, the supreme archduke who holds the ninth and deepest layer, Nessus. Portals from outside the plane arrive only at the first layer, Avernus. The **River Styx** winds through every layer, providing the only continuous route downward.

| Layer | Ruler | Character |
|---|---|---|
| **Avernus** | Zariel | Eternal Blood War battlefield; first arrival point |
| **Dis** | Dispater | Iron city of deals, contracts, and manipulation |
| **Minauros** | Mammon | Stinking bogs and cyclopean cities; greedy bargains |
| **Phlegethos** | Belial & Fierna | Volcanic obsidian fortresses |
| **Stygia** | Levistus (imprisoned in ice) | Frozen hellscape with cold flames |
| **Malbolge** | Glasya | Endless, avalanche-prone slope |
| **Maladomini** | Baalzebul | Ruined cities plagued by flies |
| **Cania** | Mephistopheles | Soul-freezing blizzards; ice-trapped cities |
| **Nessus** | Asmodeus | Bottomless pits and the great citadel-spire Malsheem |

## Inhabitants and Hierarchy

Devils exist in a rigid ranked hierarchy: lemures (soul-fragments of the evil dead) at the bottom, rising through imps, bearded devils, chain devils, bone devils, horned devils, erinyes, ice devils, and pit fiends, up to the archdevils. Promotion and demotion are tools of control wielded by superiors. Asmodeus alone can promote a greater devil to archdevil status.

## The Blood War

The Nine Hells is one of the two primary theatres of the **Blood War** — the millennia-long conflict between devils (lawful, regimented) and demons of the [[entities/the-abyss|Abyss]] (chaotic, savage). Avernus bears the brunt of the fighting on the Hells' side.

## Role in the Cosmology

As the archetypal Lower Plane of Lawful Evil, the Nine Hells is a destination for quests involving devil contracts, imprisoned souls, and the recovery of items or individuals swallowed by infernal bureaucracy. Adventurers who descend here risk corruption as much as physical danger.
