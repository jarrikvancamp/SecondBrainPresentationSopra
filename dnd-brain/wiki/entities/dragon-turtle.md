# Dragon Turtle

**Type:** Gargantuan Dragon  
**CR:** 17  
**Alignment:** Neutral

## Overview

Dragon turtles are massive reptilian predators that inhabit deep ocean waters and coastal seas. They combine a dragon's ferocity with the near-impenetrable shell of a sea turtle, making them among the most formidable aquatic creatures in existence.

## Signature Traits

- **Steam Breath:** Exhales a cloud of scalding steam in a large cone, dealing fire damage to creatures caught inside. The steam lingers briefly, obscuring sight.
- **Amphibious:** Equally capable on land and beneath the water's surface.
- **Shell:** The thick dorsal shell provides exceptional natural armor, contributing to a very high AC.
- **Bite & Claws:** Powerful melee attacks that can shred ships or large creatures.

## Ecology & Behavior

Dragon turtles are largely solitary, staking out large territories in coastal shallows or deeper ocean trenches. They have been known to demand tribute from passing ships—treating a vessel as an intruder in their domain unless appeased with gold or goods. Sailors sometimes mistake a resting dragon turtle for a small island.

Unlike true dragons, dragon turtles do not age through named categories; a single stat block covers the species. They do not use legendary actions.
