Ancient, fish-like aberration that predates the gods and hoards memories spanning eons.

**Role/type/CR:** Large aberration, CR 10. Roughly AC 17 (natural armor), ~135 HP. Possesses legendary actions and is typically encountered in deep water or flooded ruins.

**Signature abilities**
- *Mucus Cloud* — while submerged, the aboleth exudes a toxic mucus that can inflict a wasting disease on nearby swimmers, forcing affected creatures to remain wet or suffer Constitution damage.
- *Tentacle Attacks* — striking tentacles can cause a diseased condition that slowly transforms the victim's skin into a translucent, membrane-like tissue.
- *Enslave* — a powerful charm effect that places a creature under the aboleth's psychic domination; unlike most charms, this persists even when the aboleth is not nearby.
- *Probing Telepathy* — the aboleth automatically reads the surface thoughts of any creature that communicates telepathically with it.
- *Legendary Actions* — can use tentacle or psychic probe attacks outside its normal turn.

**Lore in brief**
Aboleths are effectively immortal and never truly forget anything — they inherit the memories of every ancestor upon birth, giving each one a mind that stretches back before mortal history began. They view gods and mortals alike as upstarts in a world they once owned, and they scheme across centuries to reclaim it.
