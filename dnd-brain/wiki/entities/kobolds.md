# Kobolds

**Type:** Small Humanoid  
**Alignment:** Lawful Evil (typically)

## Overview

Kobolds are small, reptilian humanoids that venerate dragons as near-deities, seeking to serve them in exchange for protection. They are industrious tunnel-builders and trap-setters, and while individually weak, they fight with surprising effect in packs.

## Variants (2024 Monster Manual)

| Variant | Approx. CR | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Kobold Warrior | 1/8 | Standard fighter; dagger and sling |
| Winged Kobold (Urd) | 1/4 | Rare winged breed; drops rocks from above |

## Signature Traits

- **Pack Tactics:** A kobold attacking a foe that has an ally adjacent gains advantage on the attack roll—rewards fighting in numbers.
- **Sunlight Sensitivity:** Kobolds are at disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks in sunlight, making them most dangerous underground.
- **Trap Expertise:** Not a stat-block trait, but a narrative staple—kobold lairs are typically riddled with pit traps and alarm systems.

## Ecology & Behavior

Kobold warrens are often found in the vicinity of a dragon's lair, where kobolds eagerly perform menial tasks in exchange for the dragon's protection. Without a patron dragon they form independent clans, typically in underground tunnels or ruined structures. They rely on ambushes, superior numbers, and prepared terrain rather than individual combat prowess.
