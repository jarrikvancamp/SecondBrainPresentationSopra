# Devils

Devils are Lawful Evil fiends from the Nine Hells of Baator. In sharp contrast to demons, devils are highly organized, hierarchical, and strategic. They pursue domination through contract, manipulation, and military order. All devils share immunity to fire and poison, resistance to cold and physical damage from non-magical weapons, and darkvision.

## The Hierarchy Concept

Infernal society is a rigid rank-and-file structure where each category of devil oversees those below it. Lesser devils serve greater ones under pain of destruction, while greater devils scheme constantly to ascend. The overall structure is ruled by the archdevils (Asmodeus at the apex), each commanding one of the Nine Hells' layers.

## The Blood War

Devils fight a perpetual war against demons for control of the Lower Planes — see [[entities/demons]]. Devils consider the war a matter of discipline versus chaos; their legions are structured, but demon numbers are near-infinite. Some diabolic strategists view the Blood War as advantageous, since it keeps demons occupied and away from the mortal world.
