Tentacle-faced psionic predators that sustain themselves by devouring the brains of other intelligent creatures.

**Role/type/CR:** Medium aberration. The baseline Mind Flayer is CR 7 (~AC 15, ~71 HP); the Mind Flayer Arcanist is CR 8 with additional wizard spellcasting. Both are dangerous mid-tier threats that excel at disabling entire groups.

**Signature abilities**
- *Mind Blast* — a recharge ability that projects a 60-foot cone of psychic energy; creatures inside must make an Intelligence saving throw or be stunned, completely shutting them down for up to a minute.
- *Tentacles* — a grappling melee attack targeting the head; a grappled creature is restrained and at risk of brain extraction.
- *Extract Brain* — instantly kills an incapacitated, grappled humanoid by pulling out and consuming its brain.
- *Psionic Powers* — innate abilities including telepathy out to 120 feet, the ability to detect thoughts, levitation, and self-only planar travel.
- *Mind Flayer Arcanist* — a variant that adds wizard spells on top of the standard psionic suite, making it a versatile controller.

**Lore in brief**
Mind flayers (also called illithids) reproduce through *ceremorphosis* — implanting a tadpole into a living humanoid host, whose body is slowly consumed and rebuilt into a new illithid over the course of days. Colonies are organized around an elder brain, a massive psionic intelligence that absorbs the minds of deceased illithids and maintains a telepathic hive network across the entire settlement.
