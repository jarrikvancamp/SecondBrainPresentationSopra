# Goblins

**Type:** Small Humanoid  
**Alignment:** Neutral Evil (typically)

## Overview

Goblins are small, cunning humanoids that dwell in caves, ruins, and the edges of settled lands. They survive through ambush, numbers, and a notable talent for evasion. The 2024 Monster Manual organizes them across four stat blocks covering minions through spellcasters.

## Variants (2024 Monster Manual)

| Variant | Approx. CR | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Goblin Minion | 1/8 (minion) | Uses minion rules; drops at any damage hit |
| Goblin Warrior | 1/4 | Standard raider; short sword and shortbow |
| Goblin Boss | 1 | Can redirect attacks onto adjacent allies |
| Goblin Hexer | ~1 | Spellcasting variant; curses and debuffs enemies |

## Signature Traits

- **Nimble Escape:** As a bonus action, goblins can Disengage or Hide—making them slippery melee opponents who dart in and out of cover.
- **Goblin Boss — Redirect Attack:** When targeted, the boss can force a nearby goblin ally to take the hit instead.
- **Goblin Hexer:** Brings basic spellcasting to goblin encounters, adding area debuffs.

## Ecology & Behavior

Goblins organize into loose bands under a boss or stronger creature (hobgoblin warlords often press them into service). Despite low individual threat, goblins in numbers and with terrain advantage can be surprisingly dangerous to low-level parties.
