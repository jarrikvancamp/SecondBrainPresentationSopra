# Dragons (True Dragons)

True dragons are divided into two families: **chromatic** (typically evil) and **metallic** (typically good). Both families share four age categories that determine size and power.

## Age Categories

| Category | Size | Approx. CR Range |
|----------|------|-----------------|
| Wyrmling | Small–Medium | 1–8 |
| Young | Large | 6–10 |
| Adult | Huge | 13–17 |
| Ancient | Gargantuan | 20–24 |

CR scales steeply with age. Adult and Ancient dragons gain **Legendary Actions** (extra actions at end of other creatures' turns) and **Lair Actions** (triggered on initiative count 20 in their home terrain). All dragons make a **Multiattack** combining bite and claw strikes, plus a **Breath Weapon** that recharges on a 5–6 roll.

---

## Chromatic Dragons — Evil Alignment

| Dragon | Breath Shape | Damage Type | Typical Habitat |
|--------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| Black | Line | Acid | Swamp / fetid marsh |
| Blue | Line | Lightning | Desert / badlands |
| Green | Cone | Poison | Dense forest |
| Red | Cone | Fire | Mountains / volcanoes |
| White | Cone | Cold | Arctic wastes |

- **Red** dragons are the most powerful chromatic species (Adult CR ~17, Ancient CR ~24) and the most arrogant.
- **White** dragons are the least intelligent and most instinct-driven (Adult CR ~13, Ancient CR ~20).
- **Blue** dragons are methodical tacticians, often found in desert lairs.

## Metallic Dragons — Good Alignment

| Dragon | Breath Option 1 | Breath Option 2 | Typical Habitat |
|--------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Brass | Fire line | Sleep gas line | Desert / warm hills |
| Bronze | Lightning line | Repulsion gas cone | Coastal cliffs |
| Copper | Acid line | Slow gas cone | Rocky hills |
| Gold | Fire cone | Weakening gas cone | Plains / hills |
| Silver | Cold cone | Paralysis gas cone | Mountains / clouds |

Metallic dragons have **two breath weapon options** and can alternate between them. **Gold** and **Silver** dragons are the most powerful of the metallics (Ancient CR ~24 and ~23 respectively).

---

## Common Dragon Traits

- **Legendary Resistance** (Adults & Ancients): can choose to succeed on a failed saving throw a limited number of times per day.
- **Frightful Presence**: all creatures within a wide radius must make a Wisdom save or be frightened.
- **Lair Actions**: unique environmental effects tied to the dragon's home terrain, activating each round.
