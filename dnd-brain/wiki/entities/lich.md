A wizard who voluntarily underwent undeath to pursue magical mastery without the inconvenience of dying.

**Role/type/CR:** Medium undead, CR 21. Roughly AC 17 (natural armor), ~135 HP. Has legendary resistance (×3/day), legendary actions, and potent lair actions. Considered a high-tier boss encounter.

**Signature abilities**
- *Phylactery* — the lich's soul is bound to a specially prepared object; if the lich is destroyed, it re-forms near the phylactery within days. The only way to permanently end a lich is to first destroy the phylactery.
- *Paralyzing Touch* — a melee attack that forces a Constitution saving throw; on failure the target is paralyzed for up to a minute.
- *Spellcasting* — casts spells as a 20th-level wizard, including 9th-level slots; typical repertoire includes high-damage and battlefield-control spells.
- *Legendary Resistance* — automatically succeeds on a limited number of failed saves per day, making it very difficult to shut down with single spells.
- *Lair Actions* — at initiative count 20 each round, can animate undead, fill areas with necrotic energy, or compel nearby creatures psychically.

**Lore in brief**
Becoming a lich is a deliberate, grueling ritual requiring the creation of a phylactery and the consumption of a special poison; only mages of immense power and ruthless ambition survive it. Most liches are obsessed with a singular long-term goal — an arcane project, revenge, or accumulating forbidden knowledge — and will sacrifice anything to see it completed.
